Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile applications published by SatSoft or developersatsoft-dev that link to this page. The exact data handled by each app may vary depending on the app's features, permissions, advertising setup, analytics setup, and purchase options.

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications collect, use, share, retain, and protect information. By using an app that links to this policy, you acknowledge the practices described here.

2. Information Our Apps May Collect or Process

Our apps are designed to collect only the information needed to provide app features, improve reliability, support advertising where applicable, and process purchases where applicable.

  • Local app data: settings, preferences, saved items, app configurations, device profiles, or other app-specific content that you create or save in the app. This data is usually stored locally on your device.
  • Feedback and support information: if you contact us, send feedback, or report an issue, we may receive the message you choose to send, your email address, and app or device context included in the message, such as app version, package name, Android version, device model, locale, and feature support status.
  • Analytics and diagnostics: apps may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand basic app usage, diagnose crashes, improve reliability, and measure performance. These services may process app events, crash logs, diagnostics, device information, app identifiers, and related technical data.
  • Advertising-related information: apps that show ads may use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) and Google User Messaging Platform. Google and its partners may process information such as device or advertising identifiers where available, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, app interactions, ad performance data, diagnostic information, consent choices, and signals used to serve, measure, personalize where permitted, and protect advertising.
  • Purchase information: apps that offer in-app purchases may use Google Play Billing. We may receive or store limited purchase-related information needed to unlock or restore purchased features, such as product ID, purchase state, order status, purchase token, and entitlement status. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • We do not require users to create a SatSoft account unless a specific app states otherwise.
  • We do not intentionally collect your contacts, photos, camera content, or precise location unless a specific app feature clearly requests and explains that access.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not collect full credit card numbers, bank account details, or other payment credentials. Purchases are processed by Google Play or the applicable app store.

4. Permissions

The permissions requested by each app depend on the features it provides. Common permissions may include:

  • Internet and network access: used for ads, analytics, diagnostics, purchase validation, support links, and external links.
  • Network state: used to support network-aware app behavior and third-party services.
  • Notifications: used only if an app provides notification features and you allow them.
  • Feature-specific permissions: some apps may request permissions such as infrared transmitter, Bluetooth, storage, media, camera, or location only when needed for the app's stated functionality.

5. How We Use Information

  • To provide app features and remember your preferences.
  • To save and restore local app content or purchased entitlements where applicable.
  • To respond to feedback, support requests, or issue reports.
  • To improve app quality, performance, stability, and usability.
  • To show, measure, and protect advertising where an app includes ads.
  • To comply with applicable laws, platform policies, and security requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

Depending on the app, we may use third-party services including:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): used to display, measure, personalize where permitted, and protect ads.
  • Google User Messaging Platform: used to request and manage advertising consent where required.
  • Firebase Analytics: used to understand app usage and improve app features.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: used to collect crash reports and diagnostics so we can fix stability issues.
  • Google Play Billing: used to process in-app purchases and restore purchased features.

These services are provided by Google or other third parties and process information according to their own privacy practices and terms. Helpful references include the Google Privacy Policy and Google Mobile Ads privacy guidance.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information ourselves. Information may be processed or shared with third-party service providers for app operation, advertising, analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, purchase processing, legal compliance, and service improvement. These providers process information under their own policies and legal requirements.

8. Advertising Choices and Consent

Apps that show ads may display personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region, your device settings, your consent choices, and Google advertising settings. Where required, an app may show a consent message before using certain advertising features. You can also manage ad personalization and advertising ID settings through your device and Google account settings.

9. In-App Purchases

Some apps may offer paid features, premium access, subscriptions, or one-time purchases. Digital purchases in apps distributed through Google Play are processed through Google Play Billing unless another legally permitted billing option is clearly presented. Prices, taxes, currencies, payment methods, refunds, and purchase history are handled by Google Play or the applicable app store. We do not receive your full payment details.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data can usually be deleted by using in-app delete options where available, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app. Feedback emails and support messages are retained only as long as needed to review and respond to your request, maintain records, and comply with legal obligations. Analytics, crash, diagnostics, advertising, and purchase-related data retention is managed by the relevant third-party services according to their settings and policies.

11. Children's Privacy

Our apps are generally intended for a broad audience and are not directed to children under 13 unless a specific app is clearly designed and published for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

12. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information handled by our apps. However, no mobile app, device, network, or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

  • You can manage app permissions in Android system settings.
